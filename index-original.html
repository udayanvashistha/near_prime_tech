Factory Planning

We design, permit, commission and franchise distributed mega-factories across the U.S., turning existing sites into turnkey 500 MWh+ production hubs. Scale rapidly with our proven Acquire → Operate → Franchise approach.

Cell Technology

Leveraging our patented Li-ion BM-LMP process, we deliver 166 Wh/kg specific energy, 360 Wh/L energy density and 2C fast-charge capability—all with UL, IEC & UN certifications for peak safety and performance.
Learn More

Energy Source

From e-vehicles and e-boats to DoD micro-grids, we engineer cells, modules and DC-blocks tailored to mission-critical and high-margin niches—commanding up to 200% premium versus commodity imports
Learn More

Supply chain

Partnering with C4V, Jabil and home-grown raw-material suppliers, we secure a 100% U.S.-made battery value chain—shielding you from overseas bottlenecks and CFIUS-driven restrictions.
Learn More

Compliance

Our environmental and engineering teams navigate every NYCRR air, water, SPDES and SEQRA permit plus federal historic-preservation, ESA, asbestos and chemical-storage approvals—getting you production-ready in under 12 months.
Learn More

Optimization

We ingest cell-to-pack data with machine-learning pipelines to predict thermal events, cut waste by 20% and accelerate quality control—ensuring every factory runs lean and fire-safe.
